NiCursor - Privacy Policy

Your privacy is super important to us!

By Nicholas R. do MW • Created with care for friends 💜

Last updated: March 12, 2026

👋 Hello!

NiCursor is a fun browser extension that lets you turn your favorite pictures into cool cursors! We want you to know that your privacy is safe with us.

🔒 What We Collect

Nothing! Zero! Nada! 🎉

NiCursor does not collect, send, or share any of your data. Everything stays right on your computer, just for you.

💾 What We Store on Your Computer

  • Your cursor pictures: The images you upload stay in your browser's local storage.
  • Your preferences: Like cursor size and which cursor you picked.
  • On/Off switch: Whether the extension is turned on.

All of this lives only on YOUR device. We never see it!

🔐 Why We Need Permissions

  • Storage: To save your cursor images and settings locally.
  • Active Tab: To apply your custom cursor to the page you're viewing.
  • All websites: So your cool cursor works everywhere! We don't read or collect anything from these sites.

🚫 No Tracking, No Ads, No Tricks

NiCursor does not use any analytics, tracking services, cookies, or advertising. We built this for fun, not for data!

NiCursor - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

NiCursor is a browser extension that allows users to customize their cursor with custom images. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

NiCursor does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data (cursor images, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local). No data is ever sent to any external server.

What We Store Locally

  • Cursor images: Images you upload are stored locally in your browser's extension storage.
  • Preferences: Your cursor size setting and active cursor selection.
  • Enable/disable state: Whether the extension is currently active.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Required to save your cursor images and preferences locally.
  • activeTab: Required to apply the custom cursor to the currently active tab.
  • Host permissions (all URLs): Required to apply your custom cursor across all websites you visit. No data from these websites is read or collected.

Third-Party Services

NiCursor does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties. There is no data to share because everything stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

NiCursor does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
